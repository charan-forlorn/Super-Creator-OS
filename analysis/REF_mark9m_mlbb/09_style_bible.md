# 09 · Style Bible (Phase 8)

## Visual DNA
- 16:9, 1024×576-class, 30fps, native dark-saturated MLBB palette, neon-on-green.
- Full HUD always visible (minimap, timer, **kill score**, KDA, skills, spells).
- Subject (player hero) + fight always frame-centered = eye anchor.
- Only camera move = **punch-in 1.15–1.30×** on action; release on lulls.
- Zero editor-added VFX; all spectacle is in-game.
- Optional light upscale/sharpen; **no creative LUT**.

## Editing DNA
- **Invisible/masked cuts** between 6–9 different matches; seams hidden in VFX + map/HUD continuity.
- Clip length **8–11 s**, one highlight (kill/standout) per clip, no exceptions.
- **1.0× speed always** — no ramps/slow-mo/reverse.
- Cold open (gameplay frame 1, no title); first reward < 8 s.
- Hard silent end card with `@handle`.
- Pacing = **flat-high comb**, reward/spike every ~9 s, no deep valleys.

## Psychological DNA
- Variable-ratio reward schedule (novel payoff per match).
- Power fantasy + competence display + status signaling.
- Open loops: rising score (macro), ult-cast→kill (micro).
- Tension→relief cycles via uncertain fights then "Kill/Immune" resolution.
- Authenticity (HUD/real-speed/native-VFX) as the trust layer.

## Narrative DNA
- **Anti-narrative plateau**, not an arc. No story, commentary, or characters beyond the
  player. Continuity illusion ("one unstoppable run") substitutes for plot.
- Stakes re-posed each clip via a fresh scoreboard.

## Audience DNA
- MLBB / SEA mobile players, hero-mains, rank-aspirational, short-session viewers.
- Want vicarious dominance + build/combo reference + identity belonging.
- Respond to: real skill at real speed, climbing scores, clean legible action.

## The 10 commandments of this style
1. Frame 1 is gameplay. No intro.
2. One highlight per 8–11 s, or cut it.
3. Make many matches look like one. Hide every seam.
4. Cut on the brightest VFX frame.
5. Punch in on the kill; never speed-ramp.
6. Keep the HUD; the score is the story.
7. Add no effects the game didn't.
8. Music starts on frame 1 and never stops till the CTA.
9. End on silent black with the @handle.
10. Real speed = credibility = watch-time.

## Do-NOT list (style-breakers)
- ❌ Visible compilation cuts / whip transitions / flash-frames.
- ❌ Slow-mo, speed-ramps, reverse.
- ❌ Editor VFX overlays (CA, shake, light leaks, particles).
- ❌ Cropping out the timer/score HUD.
- ❌ Title cards, long intros, mid-video dead-air.
- ❌ Heavy film LUT that muddies neon VFX.
